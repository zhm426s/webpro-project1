The Whitmore Gallery Case

Victim

Name: Eleanor Whitmore

Age: 29

Date of Death: March 14, 2023

Time of Death: Approximately 9:17 PM

Location: Private office at Whitmore Gallery

Background

Eleanor Whitmore was the founder and sole owner of Whitmore Gallery. She had built a strong reputation for ambitious exhibitions and aggressive business expansion. Recently she announced plans to open a second location. Despite the gallerys' public success, financial documents suggested the business was under hidden financial stress.

Circumstances of Death

  • Eleanor was found dead in her locked office during the gallery anniversary event.
  • Security cameras were disabled between 9:05 PM and 9:58 PM.
  • Cause of death: blunt force trauma to the back of the head.
  • A shattered wine glass was discovered near the body.
  • A text message sent at 8:52 PM read: “Meet me in the office. We need to settle this tonight.”
