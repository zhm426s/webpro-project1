The Murder of Isaac Harlow

The Basics:
Isaac Harlow was murdered around 11:45 PM on August 19, 2022. He was found covered in bruises and blood in his closet at around 1:00 AM on August 20, 2022 by his roommate, Nate Ribeiro.
Medical examination found that he was killed by a combination of two blows to the head from a baseball bat. There are four potential suspects who were never ruled out.

About the Victim:
Isaac was the only son of Franklin (died 2015) and Sharon Harlow. He lived with two roommates: Nate and Danny.
He had a girlfriend, Katy, and two close friends, Nate (same as roommate) and Joshua.
According to interviews with suspects and others, he was not liked very well by anyone in his life, except possibly Joshua.
