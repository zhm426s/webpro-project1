You Identified the Killer

Thomas Greene murdered Eleanor Whitmore.

Thomas had administrative access to the gallery security system and disabled the cameras at 9:05 PM.

Eleanor confronted him about his poor performance and possible termination. During the argument, Thomas struck Eleanor with a heavy marble paperweight from her desk.

After the attack, he left the office and blamed the camera outage on a system malfunction.

The missing marble paperweight, the disabled security cameras, and Thomas’s control of the system all point directly to him.

Case Closed.
